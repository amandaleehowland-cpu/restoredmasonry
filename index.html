Restored
Masonry

Heritage masonry conservation for pre-1940 buildings across the Greater Ottawa area and Prescott-Russell. Every repair tailored to its building, held to compatibility, documentation, and craft.

THE APPROACH

Understand how lime mortar behaves, how old brick ages, how moisture moves through a wall, and why the wrong repair can do more harm than the damage it was meant to fix. No two buildings are exactly alike; every repair should be tailored to its needs.

SERVICES
Lime Mortar Repointing

Traditional lime mortars matched to the original in strength, colour, and joint profile.

Stone Repair

Piecing-in, dutchman repairs, and consolidation of weathered and damaged stone.

Brick Restoration

Repair and replacement using period-appropriate, locally fired brick.

Heritage Conservation

Documentation-led rehabilitation of pre-1940 buildings, guided by compatibility.
